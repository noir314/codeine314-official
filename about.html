Operating out of the Clarksville and Nashville area, Codeine314 is an independent recording artist dedicated to authentic, direct-to-fan music distribution. From producing the initial concepts to managing this independent storefront, everything is built from the ground up. This platform serves as the central hub for all official merchandise, exclusive releases, and direct support for the music.
